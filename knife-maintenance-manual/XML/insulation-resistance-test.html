Insulation Resistance Test

  1. Perform Safety Lockout procedure in Operation section.
  2. For each cylinder motor, perform the following:
    1. Disconnect the motor armature wires from the associated cylinder drive as well as the motor ground wire from the ground bar.
    2. Use a high voltage insulation tester (Meggor) with the following Insulation Resistance Testing specifications:

      RATED VOLTAGE / RATED RESISTANCE = 1,000 V / 2,000 M ohm

      EFFECTIVE MEASURING RANGE = 2 to 2,000 M ohm

      Measure the resistance from phase to phase and phase to ground for each motor winding according to the chart below. A minimum reading of 1,000 megohm should be seen with 1,000 V applied.

    3. If any of the checks do not meet the resistance specification, record the observed measurement, the motor number, and the wire numbers. Replace the faulty motor with a spare motor and return the faulty motor to BW Papersystems (with the recorded motor information) at the next possible shutdown. Contact BW Papersystems Field Service for assistance.
