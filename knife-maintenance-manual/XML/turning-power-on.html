Turning Power On

Note:

This procedure should be used when starting a machine after removing power from safety zones or after a safety lockout.

  1. Walk around the machine to verify:
    1. All guards and safety signs are in place.
    2. No debris or tools are inside machine.
    3. People are clear of machine.
  2. Turn air on.
    1. Shout “Air on.”
    2. Pause, look, and listen.
    3. Turn air on by rotating the air disconnect valve handle.
    Note:

    Some machine motion could occur when turning on air.

  3. Turn primary and secondary disconnects on. Reference Safety Features Section for disconnect locations.
    1. Shout “Power on”.
    2. Pause, look, and listen.
    3. Stand to one side and move the primary power disconnect handle to ON. Turing this disconnect on applies power to the Knife right side drive panel, Knife left side power distribution panel, sheeter line PC, logic power supplies, air conditioner, and various power monitoring devices in the knife/stacker control cabinet provided the appropriate circuit protection devices are turned on.
    4. Stand to one side and move secondary power to disconnect handle to ON. Turning this disconnect on applies power to all other items.
  4. Reset E-Stop system.
    1. Release all E-Stop buttons.
    2. Press E-Stop Reset (if applicable).
  5. Reset slitter outfeed/knife infeed safety circuit located on the slitter operator side.
    1. Verify all doors are closed and all safety switches are reset.
    2. Verify all people are clear of the safety zone.
    3. Press the Slitter Outfeed/Knife Infeed Safety Circuit reset push button.
    4. Verify that both the Knife Cylinder Drive 3-phase Voltage Indicator and Knife Cylinder Drive Bus Voltage Indicator LED(s) are illuminated. If LED(s) are not illuminated, contact maintenance immediately to repair failed components.
