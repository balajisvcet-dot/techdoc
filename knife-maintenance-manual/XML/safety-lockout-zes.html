Safety Lockout (ZES)

Perform Safety Lockout before performing any maintenance or jam clearing. Follow Safety Lockout procedure to obtain a Zero Energy State (ZES).

DANGER

Failure to perform Safety Lockout procedure before performing maintenance or jam clearing could result in severe injury or death.

  1. Lower all lifts and other devices to their lowest possible positions (if applicable).
    DANGER

    Support lifts and other devices by their locking pins or bottom stops. Do not rely on normal stopping mechanisms only. Failure to do so could result in severe injury or death.

  2. Verify that LED(s) on High Voltage DC Bus Detect indicator are illuminated. If LED(s) are not illuminated, contact Maintenance immediately to repair failed components before proceeding with Safety Lockout.
  3. Press the Knife Outfeed Access Request push button to discharge the knife cylinder drive(s) stored electrical power. Verify the LED(s) on the High Voltage DC Bus Detect indicator darken
  4. Ensure that stored electrical power has dissipated:
    1. Wait 30 seconds.
    2. Verify that LED(s) on High Voltage DC Bus Detect indicator are dim. Stored electrical power is present when LED(s) are brightly lit.
    DANGER
    Wait until LED(s) are dim before performing any maintenance or jam clearing. Failure to do so could result in severe injury or death.
    • Cut hazard. Stored electrical energy could allow cut cylinders to rotate.
    • Electrocution hazard. Verify capacitor voltage is less than 5 V DC before performing electrical maintenance.
  5. Lock out primary disconnect.
  6. Turn air off at pneumatic lockout valve.
  7. Lock out pneumatic valve.
