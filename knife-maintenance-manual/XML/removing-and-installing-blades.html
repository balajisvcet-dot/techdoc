Removing and Installing Blades

Blade replacement is required when blades become damaged or worn beyond repair.

CAUTION:

Removal of old blades and installation of new blades can be dangerous. Before manually rotating the cylinders, check that all hands and fingers are clear. Only one person should perform installation procedures.

Note:

Use cut-resistant gloves to protect hands from sharp blades.

CAUTION:

Remove blades by removing bolts progressively from one end to the other to prevent a sudden, dangerous release of blade stress.
