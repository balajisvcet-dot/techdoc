Cylinder Motor Resolver Replacement

  1. Perform Safety Lockout procedure in Operation section.
  2. Loosen resolver coupling screws nearest the motor.
  3. Remove resolver mounting screws, and carefully pull the resolver and coupling off the mounting stand. The coupling is a flexible type and can be damaged by excessive force.
  4. Remove mounting resolver stand from the motor.
  5. Align the motor scribe marks (Figure 24).
  6. Reinstall mounting stand onto the motor.
  7. Remove the resolver cable and coupling from the old resolver.
  8. Attach the resolver cable to the new resolver.
  9. Turn Knife/Stacker cabinet main and secondary disconnects on.
  10. Turn Knife disconnect on.
  11. Locate knife cylinder drive 1 in the knife right side electrical enclosure (figure 26).
  12. Locate resolver position LED bank on cylinder drive 1 (item 3, figure 26).
  13. LED’s that are currently lit indicate how far resolver is out of phase. As resolver is rotated, LEDs shift depending on how close resolver is aligned. When both middle LEDs are green, resolver is aligned (figure 25).
  14. Rotate shaft on the resolver until both middle LEDs on the drive illuminate green (figure 25).
  15. Carefully slide coupling onto resolver shaft while avoiding shaft rotation. Tighten coupling to allow access to coupling screws when resolver and coupling are mounted.
  16. Attach resolver to mounting stand.
    Note:

    Before tightening resolver mounting screws, verify access to coupling screws as stated in Step 15.

  17. Tighten coupling screws nearest to the cylinder motor, ensuring both middle LEDs on the knife cylinder drive still illuminate green.
    Note:

    If both LEDs on the knife cylinder drive are not illuminated green, loosen fork clamp and rotate mounting stand until both LEDs illuminate green.
