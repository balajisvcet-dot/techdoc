Cylinder Drive Replacement

Figure 1. Cylinder Drive Bus Control

  1. Perform Safety Lockout procedure in Operation section.
  2. Verify the BUS indicator light is dim (Orange LED) (Item 1, figure 26).
  3. Use a multi-meter to check the bus leads (Item 2, figure 26).
  4. Check the BUS positive to neutral. Confirm the reading is less than 5V. Use a multi-meter to check the bus leads (Item 2, Figure 26).
  5. Check the BUS negative to neutral. Confirm the reading is less than 5V. Use a multi-meter to check the bus leads (Item 2, Figure 26)
  6. Disconnect all wiring (top, bottom, and front).
  7. Remove the three hex bolts on the bottom and three hex bolts on the top of the cylinder drive.
  8. Remove the cylinder drive. Note the settings of the SW1, SW2, SW3 and SW4 (Item 3, Figure 26), and set the replacement drives switches to the same settings.
  9. Attach the replacement cylinder drive with the six hex bolts.
  10. Reconnect all wiring.
  11. Set the switches to the engineering specification or match the original cylinder drive.
  12. Set the SW1, SW2, SW3 and SW4 settings as noted in item 8.
  13. Turn the power on, performing the steps int eh Turning Power On procedure in the Operation section.
