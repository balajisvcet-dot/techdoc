Gaining Access to Knife Cylinders

Warning:

When rolling out Knife Outfeed assembly, keep hands and fingers clear of all stops and pinch points.

Warning:

Wear cut-resistant gloves, long sleeves, and protective eye wear when working near knife blades.

  1. Perform Intermediate Energy State (IES)-Knife Zone procedure in Operation section.
  2. Press Knife Outfeed Access Request push button on operator station.
  3. Knife Outfeed carriage section can be moved away from the knife after bus voltage is bled down below a safe level.
  4. Perform Safety Lockout procedure in Operation section.
  5. Open access door and gain access to knife cylinders.
