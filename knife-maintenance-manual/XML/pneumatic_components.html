Description

Function

Nip Roll Switch

Spring return to center switch, that when actuated, raises or lowers the nip roll.

2

Right Side Nip Hold Down Roll Regulator

Regulates the nip roll hold down pressure going to the right side cylinders.

3

Left Side Nip Hold Down Roll Regulator

Regulates the nip roll hold down pressure going to the left side cylinders.

4

Pull Roll Brake Valves

When energized, the pull roll brake is released.
