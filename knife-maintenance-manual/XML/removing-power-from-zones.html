Removing Power from Zones (IES) (If Applicable)

DANGER

Machine zones have been defined to allow safe access to one area of the machine while allowing other areas of the machine to continue operation. The procedures below will place each zone specified in a stopped, but Intermediate Energy State (IES), while other zones may remain operational. Zones may be put in this state by the operator between periods of normal operation or when minor jam clearing and cleaning is required. Only enter a zone when it has been placed in IES state. Follow the Safety Lockout procedure in Operation section to obtain a Zero Energy State (ZES) if required.

Knife Zone
  1. Stop the machine by pushing one of the Stop buttons.
  2. Wait for the machine to come to a stop.
  3. Push the Knife Outfeed Access Request button on the Knife operator station.
  4. Access will be allowed to the knife outfeed section when the cylinder drive bus is bled down below a safe voltage. Verify the LED(s) on the High Voltage DC Bus Detect darken before entering the zone. If LED(s) do not darken, contact Maintenance immediately to repair failed components before entering zone.
