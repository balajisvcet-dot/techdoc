Pull Roll Motor Replacement

  1. Perform Safety Lockout Procedure in Operator section.
    CAUTION:

    MOTOR IS HEAVY. Use crane or forklift.

    Note:

    Custom lifting Jig may need to remove and assemble the Motor

  2. Perform steps 2-6 in topic “Pull Roll Drive Belt Replacement” in this manual.
  3. Connect motor to crane or Forklift using custom Jig. Jig is not supplied with machine.
  4. Remove motor mounting bolts, Lock nuts and Jack bolts.
  5. Remove motor from knife and assemble new motor.
  6. Assemble motor mounting bolts, lock nuts and jack bolts. Do not tighten them.
  7. Perform steps 7-9 in topic “Pull Roll Drive Belt Replacement” in this manual.
