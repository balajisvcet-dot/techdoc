  1. Lower Nip Roll (6) completely to rest on Pull Roll (7).
  2. Perform Safety Lockout Procedure in Operator section.

  3. Loosen (do not remove) the bolts (10) that holds the pillow block bearing(11). Quantity 2 on right side and quantity 2 on Left side.
  4. Loosen Lock Nuts (5).
  5. Move pillow block bearing(11) to approximately to the center in between two adjuster blocks (12) by adjusting the jack bolts (4).
  6. Position and level the jig when it rests on pull roll at B. If C contacts prior to B or gap at B, Adjust jack bolts (4) to move Nip Roll upstream or downstream until it contacts Jig at C.
    Note:

    Do not do any adjustment to Pull roll (7).

  7. Check with Jig on Right and Left sides.
  8. Tighten Lock nuts (5) and Bolts (10). Verify the roll alignment, if required repeat steps 3-8.
