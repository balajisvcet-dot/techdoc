Cylinder Encoder Replacement

  1. Perform Safety Lockout procedure in Operation section.
  2. Locate Cylinder Encoder in right side enclosure. Disconnect encoder cable from encoder.
  3. Loosen encoder coupling screw in the encoder.
  4. Remove encoder mounting screws and pull the encoder off the shaft.
  5. Slide new encoder on the shaft.
  6. Attach encoder to flange by tightening the mounting screws.
  7. Tighten coupling screws in the encoder.
  8. Reattach encoder cable.
    Note:

    Encoder does not have to be timed to cylinder position.
