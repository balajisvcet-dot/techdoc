Manual Rotation of Cylinders

Manual rotation of cylinders is required when tuning blades.

CAUTION:

Only one person should perform manual rotation procedure. To prevent injury from hand getting caught due to cylinder rotation, keep one hand on ratchet handle and one hand behind back or place both hands on ratchet handle.

Note:

Use cut-resistant gloves to protect hands from sharp blades.

  1. Review and perform Gaining Access to Knife Cylinders procedure in maintenance section.
  2. Slide ratchet handle onto rotation shaft.
  3. Remove cover plate on left cylinder gear case.
  4. Insert socket end of rotation shaft through gear case hole, and engage exposed nut.
  5. Place other end of rotation shaft in metal rest on right cylinder gear case, and slide a pin through metal rest to secure rotation shaft in place.
  6. Wrap a clean rag around rotation shaft and push into gear case hole to prevent dirt and debris from entering gear case.
  7. Manually rotate cylinders with ratchet handle.
