Pull Roll Drive Belt Replacement

  1. Perform Safety Lockout Procedure in Operator section.
  2. Open left end enclosure.

  3. Mark the location of the Brake (2) using pen or sharpie and Remove Brake Assembly along with disc (8).
  4. Locate motor (5), lock nuts (4) and Jack Bolts (3) on left side frame.
  5. Loosen bolts (do no remove) (6) which holds the motor (5).
    CAUTION:

    Motor is Heavy. Belt tension can cause sudden movement of motor when loosening the bolts (6)

  6. Loosen the lock nuts (4) and turn Jack bots to raise the motor to loosen the tension in drive belt (1). Raise the motor until the drive belt can be removed from pulleys.
  7. Install new Belt. Adjust the Jack bolt to lower the motor to create tension in the drive belt. Belt should be at proper tension as listed in the table below. Use Screw Jack between top of motor (5) and cross tube (7) to get proper tension. Screw Jack and a plate to bridge gap in the cross tube are not supplied with the machine.

  9. Assemble Disc (8) and Brake (2).
